Connect With A Therapist Who Cares

My hope is to help. Whether you are dealing with challenges as an individual, a couple, or a family, we will work together in creating the changes you seek. You may have a specific goal in mind, perhaps even a fairly good idea of how to get there, but maybe not - maybe you're only aware of what you don't want and what's not working. That in and of itself is a perfectly acceptable place to start!

My skills are diverse, but I specialize in treating relationship issues and interpersonal conflict. Prior to counseling I pursued a career in Mediation, which entailed intensive training in communication and conflict resolution. Essential skills, really, when counseling couples through turbulent times or family members with differing worldviews.

I sincerely wish you well in the process of therapist shopping (I know it can be tiresome). You may already sense from my profile here that I may or may not be a good fit for your needs - but if a brief phone call would help you get a better feel for my personality, skills, and therapeutic style, I do offer a free 15-minute phone consultation.
